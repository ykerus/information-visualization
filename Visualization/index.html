Welcome to our OmniArt visualization!

The goal of this web application is to show the ratio between male and female artists among the artists in the dataset and to show the ratio between western and non-western art among art pieces in the dataset.
The following symbols are used:

♀ female
♂ male
NW non-western
W western
? unknown

Time slider:
The slider can be used to select a time period (in years), and the diagrams will update accordingly. The slider will zoom in when the selected period is shorter than 500 years. To zoom out, move both pointers to each end of the slider.

Bar charts and donuts:
The bar charts can be clicked in order to switch between categories (male vs female and western vs non-western). The donut charts in the middle show the relative distribution of artworks within different collections, and the ratios within the selected category. If a donut is large enough, the number of artworks of that specific collection will appear in the donut hole.
